def get_geolocation(image_path): ¶

Retrieves the geolocation information from the given GeoTIFF image. The returned :class:`fiftyone.core.labels.GeoLocation` will contain the lon/lat coordinates of the center of the image in its ``point`` attribute and the coordinates of its corners (clockwise, starting from the top-left) in its ``polygon`` attribute. Args: image_path: the path to the GeoTIFF image Returns: a :class:`fiftyone.core.labels.GeoLocation`
